Header

A full width static header image element, often called a 'hero image'.

Example

A header element often comes directly after the navbar and is the full width of the page. It will resize automatically on smaller devices to stay full width.

Usage

To make sure the header image is the full width of the page and scales when the browser is made smaller, it must be contained in the <header> tag.

<header>
    <img class="header-image" src="images/image.png">
</header>
